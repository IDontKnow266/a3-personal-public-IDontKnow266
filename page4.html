Changing Tires - Summer to Winter

The final piece, I will be discussing is changing summer to winter tires!

Good Bad Fun Facts
Helps to pay attention to detail, you know what tires you're putting on and all that good stuff Harder job to do as more pieces can go wrong, it is also important to pay attention to how you put the lug nuts on I was working on a 2010 Lexus LS600HL, beautiful car but those tires were 275/30/R20, which is big and they were super heavy, I struggled to lift them
Good to check the surroundings around the tire, brakes, wheels, and suspenison components Pay attention to the tires direction if they are directional, good to rotate as well, many people mess up directional tires For example you can read some information from 275/30/R20, 275mm tire width, 30mm sidewall, and 20 inches wheels
Saves a bit of money Physically intense job compared to others, some cars have heavy tires and wheels Tires are important, in fact one of, if not the most important piece on a car, don't cheap out on them and pay attention to their tread depth
