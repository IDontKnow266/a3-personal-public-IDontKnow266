Engine Oil

One of the top four common maintenance is changing the engine oil, this one is crucial yet can be tricky!

Good Bad Fun Facts
Saves money, is an easy process to follow Overfilling or underfilling the oil is the single step that gets messed up often, it is recommended to check the specs of the car, use correct oil and to test properly Newer cars are using thinner oils as they increase fuel economy and flow better however, they do have decreased cooling capacity
You have to do this one all gas powered cars, some cars have an early or later oil change intreval but it is still required, good skill to have Stripping bolts, oil drain plugs and tedious placement of drain plugs is another big issue, can be tricky for a newbie and can cause people to get stuck, if you are not confident it is best to take it to a mechanic In winter climates, thinner oils help spread quicker around the engine to ensure protection as the car warms up
If you get good at it, you can even start changing other people's oil to make some extra cash Once again, using the incorrect filter, oil type, oil additives depending on certain vehicles, and wrong drain plug / gaskets can cause severe issues for the car Oil can tell you a lot about the state of the engine, metal shavings, dirty, color change and many more can pinpoint issues
