Wipers

One of the top four common maintenance is changing the wiper blades!

Good Bad Fun Facts
Good skill as it is a common job to do, especially in Canada where winter's are quite harsh There are select cars which have more advanced or confusing wiper hooks / links, should be left alone or carefully Mercedes-Benz have a wiper that has the washer fluid nozzles built into them, they work great but also cost about 100+ dollar per piece
Attention to detail, can add upgraded wipers or special use wipers such as snow wipers, which help in winter Proprietary wiper blade connectors, have to go to the dealership to get them Intermittent wiper blades were inspired by the blinking of the eyelids
If a wiper goes bad, it is good to do on the whim especially in rainy weather, where visibility is crucial Brittle and older wipers can crack, annoying to deal with Cleaning you windscreen and removing, bugs or filth can help improve your wiper's lifespan significantly
